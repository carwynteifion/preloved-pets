Our Campaigns

Join us in our mission to help pets in need through our various campaigns.

Current Campaigns

We run several campaigns throughout the year to raise awareness and funds for our cause. From adoption drives to fundraising events, there are many ways you can get involved and support our mission. Check out our current campaigns and see how you can make a difference.

Building a Wing for Reptiles and Fish

We are currently raising funds to build a new wing to house reptiles and fish. The initial goal to build the wing, including 1 terrarium, 2 medium-sized aquariums, and running costs, is £20,000. For every additional £20,000 raised, we have set the following stretch goals:

  • £40,000: 1 additional terrarium
  • £60,000: 2 additional large aquariums
  • £80,000: An educational centre for visitors to learn about reptiles and fish

We will post additional stretch goals if the above ones are met!

Past Campaigns

Our past campaigns have helped us achieve great things for pets in need. Learn more about our previous efforts and the impact they have had on our community. Your support has made all of this possible, and we are grateful for your continued involvement.

Adopt-a-Thon

Our Adopt-a-Thon event was a huge success, with over 100 pets finding their forever homes. We partnered with local businesses and organisations to host the event, which featured pet adoptions, educational workshops, and fun activities for the whole family.

Spay and Neuter Drive

Our Spay and Neuter Drive helped reduce the number of unwanted pets in our community. We offered free spay and neuter services to pet owners in need, helping to prevent overpopulation and reduce the strain on local shelters.
