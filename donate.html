Why Donate?

Your donations help us provide food and shelter for one of the hardest times in a pet's life. Every donation, no matter how small, makes a big difference in the lives of the pets we care for. Help us continue our mission by making a donation today.

There are several ways you can contribute. You can use the donation form on this page to make a one-time contribution, or you can contact us to:

  • Set up a recurring donation
  • Subscribe to our quarterly Supporters' Club newsletter
  • Leave a gift for us in your will
  • Donate your time by volunteering at our shelter

We accept the following modes of payment:

  • Credit/Debit Card
  • Bank Transfer
  • Cheque
  • Cash donations may only be accepted at the shelter.
