Our Work

Learn more about what we do to help pets find loving homes.

Our Mission

At Preloved Pets, our mission is to take in pets from owners who have passed away and rehome them. We provide a safe and loving environment for these pets during their transition period. Our dedicated team of volunteers and staff offer medical care, emotional support, and training to help these pets adjust and find new, loving homes.

We normally take in dogs and cats, but we also frequently take in rodents. Our latest campaign is to raise enough money to open a new wing to house reptiles and fish. If you would like to know more, take a look at our Campaigns page.

How You Can Help

Preloved Pets is entirely self-funded, relying on donations, gifts left in wills, and our supporter's club magazine. We are run by unpaid volunteers, including local vets and students, who dedicate their time and expertise to help us achieve our mission.

There are many ways you can support our work. You can adopt a pet, volunteer your time, or make a donation. You could even consider leaving us a monetary gift in your will. Every little bit helps and makes a big difference in the lives of the pets we care for.
